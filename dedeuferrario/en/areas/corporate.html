Corporate Department

Why us?

Our Department advise on public takeovers; international and cross-border mergers and acquisitions; disposals; stock exchange flotations; international equity offerings; joint ventures and strategic alliances; private equity; corporate restructurings and management buyouts, public private partnership as well as in specialist in general matters of corporate servicing.

In recent years there has been a significant increase in specialization within the corporate field. As a result, we have developed a number of sector specialist international practice groups including international trade, antitrust, communications and technology (including e-commerce and Internet); financial institutions; energy (electricity, oil and gas), transport and mining and base metals.

Our transactions practice includes a diverse local and international clientele for whom the firm provides advice concerning the formation of new businesses, debt and equity investments in new and existing ventures, project and equipment financing, and the purchase and sale of existing enterprises around the world. We frequently provide assistance in sophisticated multi-party transactions involving development and negotiation of complex institutional and multi-level investments and financings, from the inception to the completion of major transactions.

The firm also regularly provides advice and counsel on a wide variety of legal issues that arise in day-to-day business operations, whether in the capacity of "general counsel" or as a regional, local, or special counsel in connection with a particular matter. In addition, we have particular expertise in counseling entrepreneurial and start-up firms involved in emerging technologies, including the negotiation of licensing agreements and software contracts, the establishment and implementation of intellectual property protection and management policies and programs, and distribution and service agreements.
