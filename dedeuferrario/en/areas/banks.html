Banks and Financial Institutions

Why us?

The firm advices Banks and financial institutions. Among the activities carried out within this area of practice, the Firm advices its clients in trust issues, creation and legal authorization of new financial institutions, project and construction finance, imports finance, assets securitization, public debt, secured and non-secured loans, constitution of branches or subsidiaries in Argentina, leasing, debt restructuring. Besides, the firm is in charge of mortgage and pledge foreclosures procedures of several financial institutions.
