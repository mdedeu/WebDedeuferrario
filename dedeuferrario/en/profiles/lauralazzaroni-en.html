Laura Emilce Lazzaroni

Lawyer graduated from University of Buenos Aires, School of Law. She became a member of "Dedeu Ferrario, Abogados" in 2003 after working with the Judiciary of the Province of Buenos Aires in different civil and commercial courts of the Judicial Department of San Isidro.

She gives comprehensive advice about civil and commercial litigation as well as providing the advice about the writing of contracts of all kinds.

Her work focuses on the comprehensive advice on the law of entertainment and copyright, artists, performers, productions of phonograms and audio-visual productions, legal protection and procedures before the entities of collective management of rights.

At the same time, she provides business advice on consumer care law coordinating the area in all types of disputes in any of the country´s jurisdictions.

As a graduate of the University of Buenos Aires, she subsequently completed a degree in Family, Childhood and Adolescent Law.

In this field she also provides specialized counselling in matters of parenthood, parental responsibility, gestation by substitution, inheritance, among others.

She has also tutored as teacher in courses of grade at the University of Buenos Aires, School of Law.

She has participated in numerous conferences, courses, seminars and congresses of the areas in which she specializes.
